Digital Letters

Studio Poetics, 2024.

Read More

In short,

The digital letters is a project that attempted to reimagine a alternative form of digital communication. We tried to embed reaching out to loved ones in different mediums with an indicative prompt. It is not meant for everyday communication or for emergency purposes. It is a means to reach out through a specific purpose, generally appreciative, once in a while.

Why Digital Letters?

The ability to message anyone at any time juxtaposes an affordance of being present in the same space over an asynchronous reality. At times, this ability can reduce the quality of conversations because the medium itself does not take into account the context of the people talking on both ends. Calls almost start to resemble ‘tokens of care’.
