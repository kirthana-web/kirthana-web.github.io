Pebble

Studio Poetics, 2024.

Read More

In short,

The pebble is the first figment of an ecosystem of input devices morphed in the forms of pebbles like on the banks of a river. This is a part of a larger series of explorations of input devices with a character of their own.

Why Pebble?

They're irregularity and fairly uniform texture are qualities we wanted to explore for input through surface. There's a level of divergence in interacting with pebbles as each of them are unique in form. They occupy space in manner that is subtle and belong wherever they're.
