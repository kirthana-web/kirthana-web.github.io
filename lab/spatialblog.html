Spatial Blog

Academic, 2024.

Read More

In short,

Blogs are extensions of one’s experience expressed in words and etched in time on the internet. These expressions often are a means of communicating with the world, letting go of an idea and so forth. The relationship one shares with the practice of blogging is forged by a sense of yearning and liveliness. Their unique positioning in a ‘present’ social context and ‘personal’ nature coupled with the viscerality brought in by a 3-D environment could lead to new forms of meaning-making and perceptions associated with blogs.

The experience has been developed for WebVR and Head-Mounted Displays.
