In short,

These are a series of interactive installations designed and developed for different occassions. Primarily artistic expressions, conceptualised with a team of individuals specialised in different facets of realising an experience.

The Jaipur Sketch

Make sense of Jaipur’s old city through its tightly knit geography and craft heritage.

Within the street markets of Jaipur Old City, there is a rich craft heritage which has sustained for ~300 years. The origin of such an aggregation of crafts is rooted in a smart geographical placement within the city. The touchwall is an attempt to present the magnanimity and detail of it.

Watched, After & Now.

Someone’s watching over. The teachers are watching over their students during an exam, the gods are watching over people for deeds, the flight attendant is watching over passengers.

Notions of being watched over for care and maintaining civility are not new. They’ve existed in several forms reinforcing the desire to control, maintain & live in harmony. Being surveilled lends an individual vulnerable to the thickness of world’s interpretations. While in use as a means of law enforcement, surveillance could become a tool for monitoring, influencing and framing activities.

Absurdity

Produced as a part of an academic exercise that required a scaled installation to reflect our learnings around forms, spaces and their inter-relation. The installation reflects absurdity in one’s own life and puts the question of meaning at stake. If a distorted surface can perceive our identity in the most absurd manner, why do we attach meaningful values to our identity? Are we bound by meaning?
