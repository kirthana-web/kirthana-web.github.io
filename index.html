About

Welcome to this corner of the internet. This site is a collection of some of the things I think, do, feel and care about. I'm an interdisciplinary design student based out of India. As a design-er, I enjoy and value being able to understand a situation deeply and broadly before doing something in/to the situation.

In the past few years, I developed a keen interest in learning the ways of people, technologies, objects & societies. Parallelly, experimentation, calculated risks, thought ladders, analogical, and metaphoric leaps have always been an essential component of how I as an individual, function. With time, I started 'making' things informed by these lenses. My interests in Human-Computer interaction is rooted here. During my time at university, I've met and extensively worked with some super skilled and thoughtful peers and mentors.
